    How To Throw A Javelin

    <

    Grip

    < <

    In this image you will see the proper way to hold a javelin. Pinching right about the grip and LIGHTLY wrapping your hand around the rest of the grip.

    <

    Arm Positioning

    < <

    This shows how to properly hold your arm. The Javelin should stay level with your eyes and should remain very close to your face, almost touching it.

    <

    The Finish

    < <

    To end your throw there is what we call a "block". This is where you bend your dominant leg and straighten your non dominant leg ahead of you. The image above shows how this is done.
